###Experiment 14: Test Coverage using JaCoCo

* 12th cont. 
* jacoco plugin added in pom.xml
 <plugin> 
        <groupId>org.jacoco</groupId> 
        <artifactId>jacoco-maven-plugin</artifactId> 
        <version>0.8.11</version> 
        <executions> 
          <execution> 
            <goals> 
              <goal>prepare-agent</goal> 
            </goals> 
          </execution> 
          <execution> 
            <id>report</id> 
            <phase>test</phase> 
            <goals> 
              <goal>report</goal> 
            </goals> 
          </execution> 
        </executions> 
      </plugin>

* mvn clean test --> 100% coverage 
* remove test methods in Test.java --> coverage % reduces
